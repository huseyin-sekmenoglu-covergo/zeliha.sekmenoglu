Glum's Umbrella

This story teaches children the value of self-confidence and helping others while also reminding adult readers of the power of simple acts of friendship and understanding. Starting in a humble cabin on the outskirts of the city, the tale offers us all a little gift of hope: no matter how unhappy we may feel, with the right support and perspective, we can always find happiness.
